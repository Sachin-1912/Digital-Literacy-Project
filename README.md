Digital literacy is a very important skill in today’s world where technology is everywhere. It means knowing how to use digital tools, apps, and platforms in a smart and effective way for communication, learning, and work. This project, as part of the Digital Literacy course, was created to help students learn how to use the digital world in a safe and productive manner.

The project included five different tasks, each focusing on a key area of digital literacy. These tasks involved making an awareness infographic, creating a professional digital portfolio, exploring coding and teamwork platforms, practicing how to write professional emails, and learning about cybercrime and online safety. Through these activities, students gained useful skills that can support their studies and help them in their future careers.
